About HHUP
what is HHUP?
HHUP is short for "Hamburg unsere Perle" (it refers to a song), the title of the Hamburg CouchSurfing Invasion from August 7th till August 9th 2015. This year will be the fourth time this event takes place with an expected number of 200 to 250 participants from all over the world. Join us for a weekend of fun, party, culture, new friends and much more! sign up now
program
During the weekend we have a variety of activities for you. Please make sure you click 'join' on the activities you want to participate in, once you are signed up for HHUP. Some of the activities are limited.check out the program
pricing
Like the last years, we gave and give our best to make this weekend as cheap as possible for you and yet make it an awesome experience. Thus we have to ask you for a fee. It's
  • 10€ if you pay upfront trough bank transfer,
  • 10,72€ if you pay through paypal (0,72€ paypal fees) or
  • 15€ if you pay cash on site because we want to encurage you to pay upfront.
The fee includes:
  • a beautiful wristband
  • free entrance for our main venue on Saturday
  • two drink vouchers, worth 3€ each for the main party on Saturday
  • all activities (unless it says otherwise in the activity description)
  • stuff we bring along for the different picnics and barbecues (grills, coal, soundsystem, torches ...)
Please visit the payment page to make your payment. to view it, please login.view payment page
who can come?
Anyone! Although we call it a CouchSurfing happening it does not mean, that it is limited to users of the CS platform. Anyone can come and everyone is invited! Please note, that we are not affiliated with Couchsurfing International inc., we just consider ourselves CouchSurfers as in hospitality exchangers in the classical way. sign up now
accomodation
